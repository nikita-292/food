Recipe Name: Poha

Estimated Calorie Intake: Approximately 200 calories per serving

Health Benefits: Poha is a nutritious and light breakfast option. It is made from flattened rice, which is easily digestible and provides a good source of carbohydrates. Poha is rich in iron, vitamins, and essential nutrients. It is also low in calories and fat, making it a healthy choice for a morning meal.

Detailed Instructions:

  1. Rinse poha under cold water and let it soak for 10-15 minutes until it becomes soft. Drain excess water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and chopped green chilies.
  3. Add diced onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  4. Add curry leaves, turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  5. Now, add the soaked poha and gently stir to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Cover the pan and let it cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Squeeze some fresh lemon juice over the poha and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the delicious and nutritious Poha for breakfast!
