Recipe Name: Oatmeal

Estimated Calorie Intake: Approximately 150 calories per serving

Health Benefits: Oatmeal is a nutritious and heart-healthy breakfast option. It is rich in fiber, which helps in maintaining a healthy digestive system and promoting satiety. Oats also contain beta-glucans, a type of soluble fiber that may help in lowering cholesterol levels. Additionally, oatmeal provides a good source of energy, vitamins, and minerals.

Detailed Instructions:

  1. Bring water or milk to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Add rolled oats to the boiling liquid and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  3. Stir continuously and cook for about 3-5 minutes until the oats are soft and the mixture has thickened.
  4. Add a pinch of salt and sweeten according to your preference with honey, maple syrup, or fruits.
  5. Top with your favorite toppings such as sliced fruits, nuts, or seeds for added flavor and texture.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy a nutritious bowl of oatmeal to start your day!
