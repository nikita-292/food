Recipe Name: Sambhar Vada

Estimated Calorie Intake: Approximately 150 calories per serving

Health Benefits: Sambhar Vada is a nutritious dish that provides a good source of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. The combination of lentils in sambhar and the fermented aspect of vada makes it a wholesome and satisfying meal. It is also rich in antioxidants and has digestive benefits.

Detailed Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal and rice separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind soaked urad dal to a smooth batter. Similarly, grind soaked rice to a coarse batter.
  3. Mix both batters, add salt, and allow it to ferment for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  4. For sambhar, prepare a mix of vegetables and cook with tamarind water, sambhar masala, and spices.
  5. Make small vadas from the fermented batter and deep fry until golden brown.
  6. Soak the vadas in warm water for a few minutes and then add them to the prepared sambhar.
  7. Allow it to simmer for a while so that vadas absorb the flavors of sambhar.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves and a side of coconut chutney.
