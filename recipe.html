Recipe Name: Pulav

Estimated Calorie Intake: Approximately 250 calories per serving

Health Benefits: Pulav is a wholesome and balanced dish that provides a mix of carbohydrates, protein, and essential nutrients. The combination of rice and vegetables makes it a nutritious and satisfying option. Pulav is also a great way to incorporate various vegetables into your diet, promoting overall well-being.

Detailed Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain the water and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Now, add a mix of vegetables like carrots, peas, and beans. Sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Add soaked and drained basmati rice to the vegetables. Stir gently to coat the rice with the aromatic mixture.
  7. Season with salt, garam masala, and other spices of your choice.
  8. Add water in the ratio of 1:2 (1 cup rice to 2 cups water). Bring it to a boil.
  9. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  10. Fluff the pulav with a fork, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot.
